About Me

After graduating from St. John's University, I set out to start my own business with my B.S. in Communication Arts in my hand, my eyes to the sky, and a glint of hope in my heart.

Of course, my first business failed – and the second, and the third. But while others will count them as failures. I see them as lessons. Beautiful, shiny, expensive lessons in all their glory. After many ups and downs, I realized the business experience I've gained was worth its weight in gold bitcoin. Then, I decided my next step would be to learn how to code!

My passion for web development grows everyday as I keep learning new things and branching my world. I hope to construct creative solutions with other like-minded individuals who are passionate about their interests. I think together, we can create beautiful things with great utility.
